Prabhat Rural Development Society
Vision

Socio, Economic empowerment of Rural, Urban, Agricultural Labourers, small and marginal farmers and other vulnerable section of the society through development and management of natural resources with special focus on health, education for an ultimate achievement of better society.

Mission

Mobilization of Urban and rural people below poverty line, build up awareness and capacitate them through networking in accessing the Government welfare programmes related to health and education with Gender conciseness.

Aims & Objectives

To generate awareness among the farming community about ill-effect of chemicals in farming and the importance of Organic farming and motivate the community to adopt organic farming techniques.

To develop systems of people’s management over common pool resources through grater accessibility and involvement of SC,ST and resource less poor for their improvement of livelihood.

To mobilize the rural communities to become pro-environment and develop in the sense of protection through social regulations and rebuild ecological balance..

To promote rural development activities, skill trainings(Tailoring and Computor Education etc) and Income generation programmes .

Capacitate the CBOs to work unitedly to achieve the results of the welfare programmes to the best advantage of target groups.

To build up awareness in the parents for the bright future of the children through education.

To build up awareness on Health and Education.

To ensure Gender equity in every sphere of life.

To establish, maintain or grant aid to homes for the aged, orphanages or other establishments for the relief and help to the poor, needy and destitute people, orphans, widows and aged persons.

To undertake service projects i.e cleaning the selected areas under new garbage recycling system.
