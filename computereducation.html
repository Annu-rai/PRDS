Computer Training

PRDS has identified the essence of the computer education in rural areas. Accordingly, it is running a computer centre at Kothacheruvu, which is one of the most prime requirements in the present society for the youth to find an employment. This is largely attended by the girls coming from the socially and economically backward Communities can come and learn skills in computer.

It is a fact that these trainings are very expensive if these students want to learn in the private institutes and the quality of teaching is also not to the expected level in small towns like Kothacheruvu. This course is of 6 months duration and includes basics of computers to exclusive class-room teaching and on-hands sessions in Operating System, Word Processing, Desktop Publishing software.

Since 2017, PRDS has started the course of Tally keeping in view the demand from the students who have completed their degree in Commerce (B.Com). However, several non-commerce students have also joined this course and getting trained in Tally ERP-9.

The beneficiary girls are given practical training for 2 hours in the lab along with 2 hours of class room teaching every day. They have to attend fortnightly tests to assess the level of learning so that any students feeling it difficult or not in a position to understand the classes can be given special attention to get the course contents perfectly.

Certificates were distributed for the candidates who have successfully completed the 6 months course.
