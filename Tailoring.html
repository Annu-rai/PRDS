Training in Tailoring

With an exclusive focus on women and adolescent girls to find employable skills, either as an individual entrepreneur or to find employment to make a sizeable income for the family.

The training in Tailoring course was started in the year 2010 in the head office premises of PRDS. However, keeping in view the demand from the women and young girls, we have added two more centres. Thus, PRDS has now got 3 Tailoring Training Centres, one each in (1) Kothacheruvu; (2) Puttaparthi; and (3) Bukkapatnam.

These trainees come from economically poor families and are not in a position to buy the raw material. The women are imparted with skills in stitching and sewing, they are ready to take-up any other assignment related to making of articles in the field of tailoring, i.e., stitching and sewing. It is a pleasure for PRDS to mention that a few trained women are engaged by a few Ready-Made Garment entrepreneurs, on a contract basis, to make different types of women wear for different age-groups. A few other trainees are working individually to make an additional sustainable income for their families.

Distribution of Sewing Machines

With the support of our humane donors, PRDS is distributing sewing machines, to 15 to 20 trained women, coming from the poorest of the poor families.
